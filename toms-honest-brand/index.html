Toms' Honest Brand

A Study in Cohesive Branding It's a seeming dichotomy. Today's consumer, especially millennials and Gen Z, are shunning the so-called experiential travel products, pre-packed and hard sold to them through traditional media. And yet, they easily adopt certain brands as icons of self-expression. It's how they identify with which group they belong.
